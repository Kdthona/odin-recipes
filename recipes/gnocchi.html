Gnocchi

Gnocchi is a tender form of pasta that is made using potatoes unlike traditional pasta that uses a dough composed of flour,water, and occasionally eggs.

Gnocchi is a tender and delicious pasta that goes well with rich and creamy sauces.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium russet potatoes, scrubbed
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Large egg
  • 1 teaspoon of kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Place potatoes in a saucepan with covered in 2in of salted water.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat and then let simmer until tender for about 30 min and then drain the water.
  3. Once cool, peel the potato skins and rice or mash the potatoes into a bowl and wait for 10 min.
  4. Fold in flour,egg, and salt until mixed and the dough holds together.
  5. Knead the dough once or twice until smooth and then divide into 4 even portions.
  6. Roll the portions into long ropes about 3/4in thick.
  7. Proceed to cut the ropes every 1/2in. Now the gnocchi is ready to cook for a few minutes in boiling water before being combined with the sauce of your choice.
