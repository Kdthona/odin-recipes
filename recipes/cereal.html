Cereal

Cereal is a classic option for breakfast in America that can contain tons of healthy fiber and minerals to start your day

Ingredients

  • Cereal Brand of your choice
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Prepare a bowl and pour the amount of cereal you want to eat into it.
  2. Pour the desired amount of milk into the bowl.
  3. Your cereal is ready to eat! :)
