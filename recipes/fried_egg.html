Fried Egg

Fried eggs are delicious, crispy, and rich additions to breakfast, lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large egg
  • 2 tablespoons of a neutral oil

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet to medium heat.
  2. Place the oil in the pan.
  3. Crack the egg into the pan being careful not to break the yolk if possible.
  4. Gently break up the egg whites surrounding the yolk with a fork. This will allow those whites to cook quicker and more evenly with the rest of the egg.
  5. Optionally, you can also add some water to the skillet and cover for the steam to help cook the whites.
  6. Once the whites are cooked, and the edge of the egg is crisp to your liking, remove the egg from the pan.
